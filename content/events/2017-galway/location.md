### Event Location

Devopsdays Galway 2017 will be held at <a href="http://www.radissonhotelgalway.com" target="_blank">Radisson Blu Galway</a>, using the Inis Mor Ballroom on the ground floor for the conference. Radission Blu Hotel & Spa Galway is ideally located near the centre of Galway City, also known as the "City of the Tribes", located only a few minutes walk from the train and bus stations.

### Getting to Galway

Galway is serviced by Shannon and Knock airports which are 1 hour away, and also by Dublin Airport via extensive bus services from Dublin airport to Galway city centre in 2.5-3 hours.

There are also Bus services from the major cities Cork, Limerick & Belfast (though Belfast is mostly via Dublin). Train is also available withe train station only 5 minutes away from the conference location.
