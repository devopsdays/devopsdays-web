Stephen Nelson-Smith is a technical delivery coach and practice lead specialising in production engineering. Since founding Atalanta Systems in 2007, he has helped dozens of clients build and operate digital products.

He currently consults for Ticketmaster International, as practice lead for Site Reliability Engineering, where he has built a new SRE team, delivered a major cloud migration for a core legacy application, been instrumental in moving the flagship application to Amazon’s managed Kubernetes service, EKS.

He pioneered the idea of Test-driven Infrastructure through speaking, training and consulting engagements, in partnership with Chef, and authored two editions of the O’Reilly book Test-driven Infrastructure with Chef. This sowed the seeds for ‘Compliance as Code’, and the influential Inspec and Test Kitchen products.

He has first hand experience of industry burnout and recovery, and is passionate about bring ideas from psychology and philosophy into the tech industry, to improve effectiveness at the same time as safeguarding health.
