Community focused technologist with a passion for enabling better outcomes for society through technology. Currently run the Serverless London User Group, co-run ServerlessDays London and am part of the ServerlessDays Global team. For his contribution to the community Ant has been recognised by Amazon Web Services as an AWS Serverless Hero.

Previously was a co-founder of A Cloud Guru and ran the first ServerlessConf in New York in 2016.  For money these days I'm a freelance consultant helping organisations bring new products to market using Serverless technology.

When not doing all things Serverless, Ant spends his time trying to keep up with his two dogs.
