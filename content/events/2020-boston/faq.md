<a name="StillHappening"><h3>1. Is DevOpsDays Boston Still Happening?</h3>
Yes! DevOpsDays Boston 2020 is still happening. All content and programming will
take place online.

<a name="When"><h3>2. When is DevOpsDays Boston?</h3>
The keynote, talks and breakout sessions will take place virtually on Tuesday,
September 29th. Stay tuned for updates to our agenda.

<a name="Schedule"><h3>3. When will the schedule be available?</h3>
The finalized agenda will be published here. Please check back for updates, as
they will be provided on an ongoing basis.

<a name="Where"><h3>4. Where is DevOpsDays Boston?</h3>
Join us from your mobile device or laptop. In alignment with best practices laid
out by the CDC and WHO, the large scale in-person component in Boston has been
cancelled.

<a name="Admission"><h3>5. How do I get into the event?</h3>
If you've already registered, click the link you were emailed by
boston@devopsdays.org. If you haven't registered for the event yet, you can do
so [here](https://hopin.to/events/devopsdays-boston-2020/).

<a name="Donations"><h3>6. Where are the proceeds donated?</h3>
All proceeds will be donated to nonprofits supporting underrepresented
communities in tech.

<a name="Open"><h3>7. When do doors open?</h3>
Our virtual “doors” will open to attendees at 9 AM EDT / 1PM GMT on September
29th and will remain open until the following day to accommodate attendees
located across the world.

<a name="Sponsors"><h3>8. Can my company sponsor DevOpsDays Boston?</h3>
If you’re interested in sponsoring DevOpsDays Boston, please check out our
[Sponsor FAQ](https://devopsdays.org/events/2020-boston/sponsor/#FAQ).

<a name="COC"><h3>9. What is your code of conduct?</h3>
We want everyone at DevOpsDays Boston to have a great time! Please help us out
by following our [Code of Conduct](https://devopsdays.org/events/2020-boston/conduct).
