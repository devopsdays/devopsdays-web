Dotan lives at the intersection of technology, product and innovation. With over 20 years in the hi-tech industry as a software developer, a solutions architect and a product manager, he brings a wealth of knowledge in cloud computing, big data solutions, DevOps practices and more. Dotan is an avid advocate of open source software, open standards and communities. Currently working as a developer advocate at Logz.io, Dotan evangelizes on Observability in IT systems using popular open source projects and standards such as ELK stack, Grafana, Jaeger and OpenTelemetry.
