Burnout is dangerous. Even more so when we can’t see the physical indicators due to being a distributed team (particularly in the COVID era). In this lighting talk I discuss the 3 data-driven burnout indicators I use to make sure my team rested and happy.

As a manager it’s your responsibility to identify employee burnout and make sure your dev team is rested and healthy. But identifying signs of burnout as a distributed team can be challenging. In this lightning talk I discuss the 3 data-driven burnout indicators I use to make sure my team rested and happy.
