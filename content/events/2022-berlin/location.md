## GLS Campus Berlin.

<p>
The GLS Campus is conveniently located in the heart of Berlin, on the popular Kastanienallee in the Prenzlauer Berg neighbourhood. Two U-Bahn-Lines (U2+U8) and two Tram lines (M1+M12) very close toi the venue makes transport from anywhere in Berlin very easy and fast. Questions? There is more information on the <a href="https://www.gls-campus-berlin.de/en/location">GLS Campus Website</a>.
