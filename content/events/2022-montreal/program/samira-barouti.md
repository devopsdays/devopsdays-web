The Operator concept was introduced in 2016 by the CoreOS. Operators have quickly become a popular pattern. They are used to automate the lifecycle of a software they manage in a cloud-native way by taking over the tasks that were traditionally performed by human operators. In this talk, I will explore how the Operator pattern could be used to integrate cloud-native applications.
