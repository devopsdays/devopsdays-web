##### Developer Advocate @ Equinix Metal

Fen is a Developer Advocate at Equinix Metal and a periodic organizer for DevOpsDays Hartford, NYC and Boston. Passionate about Resilience Engineering and Mental Health in the tech industry, they believe that every technology problem is ultimately, when you get right down to it, a people challenge. Find them @CrayZeigh on twitter or @crayzeigh@hachyderm.io on mastodon for a grab bag of tech, politics, queerness and whatever the vibes bring
