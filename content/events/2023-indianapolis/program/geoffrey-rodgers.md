The mainstream launch and acceptance of Large Language Model "chatbots",
like OpenAI's ChatGPT or Google's Bard, pave the way for potentially
significant changes to the way people perform their jobs or generally
search for information. This applies to all disciplines, far beyond just
the DevOps and technology fields.

I'll be discussing how we got here, useful ways I've taken advantage of
these tools, and some of the more interesting replies these tools have
generated, both for myself and for others.
