Aaron Soellinger is a freelance individual contributor software engineer
specializing in building machine learning and artificial intelligence
systems. Aaron has worked across diverse domains such as finance,
predictive maintenance, sports and e-commerce. Currently, Aaron is a
senior research engineer in the 3D vision team at S3G
(https://www.s3global.com/science-eng.html) developing a real-time 3D
multi-camera motion capture system for tracking balls and players for
professional soccer. Aaron also leads AI for Shoebox
(https://apps.apple.com/us/app/shoebox-sports-cards/id1580090666) in
developing deep learning based vision systems for appraising collectible
sports cards.
