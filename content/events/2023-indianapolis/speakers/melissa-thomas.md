Melissa Thomas is a maestro of project management, conducting success in
the IT function across multiple industries. Currently at GitHub remotely
in Indianapolis, Melissa's expertise as a certified Project Management
Professional, Agile Certified Practitioner, and Scrum Master sets the
stage. Melissa's passion and talent create harmonious project
performances. She mentors with Women of Project Management and is a
member of the Project Management Institute Indiana Chapter. When not
orchestrating projects, Melissa enjoys books, fitness, travel, and live
entertainment. She is a loving spouse and proud mother of four.
