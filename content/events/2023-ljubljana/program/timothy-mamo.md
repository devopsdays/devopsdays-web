The 2003 diamond heist underscores the inadequacy of traditional security in the cloud-native landscape. Adopting Distributed, Immutable, and Ephemeral (D.I.E.) infrastructure fortifies security by raising the bar for attackers. This approach, coupled with controlled chaos and continuous learning, promotes a more secure and adaptable development environment.
