As agriculture becomes increasingly reliant on technology, the need for reliable and efficient software development practices has become more important than ever. In this talk, I will share how our agriculture tech company leverages cloud-native tools such as GitHub, Kubernetes or Argo to support our DevOps practices deployed on cloud providers.
