Andrew is Founder and CEO of https://control-plane.io, where he specialises in securely unlocking next-generation cloud technologies for highly regulated clients. He holds pro bono positions as CISO at OpenUK and co-chair of the CNCF’s Technical Advisory Group for Security. His clients include Google, UK Government, Citibank, JP Morgan Chase, PWC, BP, Visa, British Gas, The Economist, and News UK. He is co-author of Hacking Kubernetes (O’Reilly Media, 2022), and has published training material and numerous whitepapers for clients including the Linux Foundation and SANS.
