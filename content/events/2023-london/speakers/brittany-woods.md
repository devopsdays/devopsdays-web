Brittany Woods is an automater of things based in London. During her career in technical and leadership roles spanning the financial, automotive, and retail sectors, Brittany has been a significant advocate for utilising DevOps practices to empower teams, increase innovation, and improve the developer experience while amplifying business outcomes.

When she's not automating the things, Brittany enjoys new adventures, clarinet and bass guitar playing, and lazy weekends.

Brittany is currently a Senior Engineering Manager for The LEGO Group.
