Sandro Cirulli is a senior consultant at The Scale Factory, a cloud infrastructure consultancy based in the UK. In his day job Sandro helps SaaS businesses leveraging the cloud but he's also concerned about the ecological impact of cloud computing so he'd like to share his experience on how businesses can reduce their carbon footprint in the cloud.
