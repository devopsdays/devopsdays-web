Sorrel is a learning specialist with 15+ years experience supporting learning in the education and technology sectors. She currently operates as a solo consultant, offering ‘Learning as a Service’ - a range of services aimed at supporting the continuous learning and development of individuals, teams and whole organisations in the technology sector. Drawing inspiration from modern software engineering practices and principles, Sorrel brings together her experiences as a researcher, educator, coach and systems thinker to create flexible and person-centred learning solutions.
