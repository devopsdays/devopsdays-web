Inma Jimenez Garcia is a Senior Technical Recruiter & Sourcing Program passionate about IT. From the beginning of her career, she focused on IT Recruiting and gradually specialised in innovation and implementation of new recruitment strategies from a data-driven perspective.
Inma is currently part of the Talent Acquisition team at Nexthink and works closely with the engineering department to support them in growing their teams by recruiting the best talent.
She also has a learning mindset and completed training in Python and Data Science recently.
