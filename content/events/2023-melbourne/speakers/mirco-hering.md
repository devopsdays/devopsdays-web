Mirco is a Managing Director at Accenture and looks after the Global DevOps practice. He supports major public and private sector companies in Australia and overseas in their search for efficient IT delivery and delivers complex technology transformation programs. 
He shares his experiences on http://notafactoryanymore.com and while speaking at international conferences. He is the author of "DevOps for the Modern Enterprise", a book helping companies adopt Agile & DevOps and push through the transformation barriers caused by legacy technology and legacy thinking. The book has been awarded as best DevOps book 2018 by DevOps.com. His conference talks have been nominated for DevOpsDozen awards in the past.
