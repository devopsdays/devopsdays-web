The overarching goal of managing infrastructure is to ensure the components are able to coexist in an environment that supports both new technology and legacy. Quickly adopting tools based on trends and disruptive technology without being accountable to how it will affect the current infrastructure leads to chaos within our systems or for the sake of the analogy generational conflict.
