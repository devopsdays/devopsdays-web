Cristina is a Founding Engineer at Cortex, an internal developer portal that enables engineering teams to drive adoption of developer best practices, funded by IVP, Sequoia Capital, and YCombinator. Prior to joining Cortex, Cristina led an engineering team at Bridgewater Associates, where she developed a portfolio analytics platform for the largest institutional investors in the world. In her free time, she loves to find cool restaurants and try to remake their recipes, learn about natural wines, and dance to Colombian music.
