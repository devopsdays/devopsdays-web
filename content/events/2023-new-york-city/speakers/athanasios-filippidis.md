Athanasios Filippidis is a Founding/Senior Software Engineer of BastionZero, a cybersecurity startup simplifying passwordless access to kubernetes, server, web, and database infrastructure.

He holds a graduate degree in Cybersecurity from Boston University. He has been working as a full-stack engineer in the cybersecurity space for several years and specializes in authentication/authorization and integrations. Lately, he has been exploring ways to harden CI/CD security.

Outside of work, he loves traveling, photography, freestyle skiing and pretty much any other high-adrenaline sport.
