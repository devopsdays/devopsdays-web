Speakers = ["laura-wickett"]

Dealing with the finances for an event is usually the least favorite part of the job. Sponsors don't pay, they want you to sign contracts, or the onboarding process will test your patience like nothing else. And that doesn't even touch dealing with expenses, deposits, and approval workflows - finances can be a confusing part of any event.

In this talk, we'll look at some of the common pitfalls faced by DevOpsDays teams and how to avoid them, and cover the services provided by Conference Ops.
