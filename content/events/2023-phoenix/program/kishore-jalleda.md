As Generative AI continues to evolve and shape the technology landscape, the engineering field is undergoing significant changes. From DevOps to Platform Eng, SRE to IT Ops, and QE/QA, the impact of AI cannot be ignored.

In this talk, we will discuss the potential impact of Generative AI on these professions, exploring how AI can augment human skills and enhance performance in these roles. We will delve into the evolution of AI from Generative AI to Orchestrator AI to Persistence AI, and the opportunities this presents for professionals in the field.

Additionally, we will examine the importance of developing “Platform Competence” and the need for next-generation platform/devops tooling to stay competitive in the industry. We will explore how professionals in the field can leverage AI to automate tasks, optimize workflows, and improve efficiency.

Moreover, we will discuss the evolving role of leaders and the skills required to lead and succeed in a future dominated by AI. We will explore the challenges and opportunities presented by the integration of AI into organizational structures, and discuss strategies for effectively navigating this transformation.

Join me for an insightful and thought-provoking discussion on the future of engineering and the impact of AI on DevOps, Platform Eng, SRE, IT Ops & QE/QA. Gain valuable insights into the potential areas of growth and opportunity for professionals in the field, and explore practical strategies for driving innovation and progress in your organization.
