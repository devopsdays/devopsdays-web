The technology landscape is forever changing and this is also reflective within the security field.  As such, it is very overwhelming for people trying to break into the security domain and it is no difference for people who actually works in it. In this talk, I will share my perspective with how I had tackled this problem, how I drowned in the tsunami of information which impacted me mentally and tips for you to consider taking your first steps with your Continuous Learning journey.
