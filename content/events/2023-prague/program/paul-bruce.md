In this presentation, a fellow DevOpsDays organizer from Boston will share their experiences growing the practitioner community, challenges during and after pandemic, and new ways of collaborating together. You'll learn:

- What we encountered while keeping the DevOpsDays Boston event alive during the pandemic
- How we came back to in-person in 2022, what we learned
- What our 2023 strategy is for re-igniting our DevOps community
- About our organizing pool and community growth models
- How you can do these things too in your geo-region!

Learn how DevOps is alive and thriving in the Boston tech scene from 2018-2023.
