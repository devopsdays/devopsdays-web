Piet is a Cloud Transformation Consultant at OpenValue. He loves to help organisations map and exploit business opportunities using technology. Is he an architect, an engineer, an advisor, or a teacher? Yes.

Piet loves to help others by blogging, writing whitepapers, lecturing, training, or speaking on stage at conferences like DevOpsDays, AWS re:Invent, Codemotion, and a variety of Meetups. He spreads knowledge about cloud migration and modernisation, skills development, and software technology. Besides being a speaker, he is an active software community member: he is one of the Community Leaders of the Dutch AWS User Group and an AWS Community Builder.
