Adopting a Zero Trust model to secure your containerized apps in distributed Kubernetes environments on premises, at the edge, and in the cloud has its benefits. However, app developers often see tight security controls as a barrier to agility. Join this session to learn how to architect Zero Trust security for Kubernetes apps in a unified, non-disruptive, and developer-friendly way.
