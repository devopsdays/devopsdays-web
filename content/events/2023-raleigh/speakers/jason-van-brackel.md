Jason van Brackel is a Unicorn Engineer at Defense Unicorns. When not falling off of skateboards, he enjoys helping people solve hard problems with code and Kubernetes.  He was the founder and former organizer of Kubernetes Philly, and a regular speaker in events in the Kubernetes and .NET communities.  He is always up for helping people with new technology and you can find him on Twitter, Twitch and LinkedIn.
