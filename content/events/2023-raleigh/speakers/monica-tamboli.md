Monica Tamboli is a seasoned professional with over 20 years of experience in the Java ecosystem. She leads the System Verification Test (SVT) team for IBM's WebSphere Hybrid Edition and specializes in implementing CI/CD practices for test applications in containers on Kubernetes and OpenShift on private and public Clouds. A passionate speaker, Monica enjoys sharing her expertise in modern technologies at various conferences and STEM events for schools.
