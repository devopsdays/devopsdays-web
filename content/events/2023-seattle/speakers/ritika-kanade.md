Software Engineer @ Apple

Ritika is a Software Engineer at the iCloud Content team at Apple in Seattle. Before she started at Apple, she was a Software Engineer at Rakuten in Fukuoka, Japan for ~3 years. She was an Openlab Intern at CERN's IT-DB department in the summer of 2015. She is well travelled and enjoys learning new languages and cultures. She believes in equal opportunities and strongly advocates for education to all.
