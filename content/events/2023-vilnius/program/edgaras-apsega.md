Cybersecurity has become an increasingly important topic in today’s interconnected world. With the rapid increase of technologies connected to the internet, number of private and state sponsored hacking groups are growing as well, we are becoming more vulnerable to cyber threats, including malware injection and data breaches. In this talk I’ll be covering how we can protect our companies software supply chain using SLSA framework and provide real world attack examples for every part of the supply chain.
