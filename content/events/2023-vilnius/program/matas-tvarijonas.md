We as infrastructure/DevOps/SRE engineers are helping to scale and maintain application infrastructure. In this talk I will cover, what we, as engineers, should ask developers to provide, to help us to maintain and scale applications. During this talk I will share my experience working in large scale startups and helping to serve millions of users every day.
