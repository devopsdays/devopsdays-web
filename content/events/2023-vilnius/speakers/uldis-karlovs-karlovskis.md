DevOps coach

Uldis Karlovs-Karlovskis is a DevOps coach with almost 20 years in the field who holistically looks at software organization and helps to understand the most valuable opportunities for improvement in a scalable and sustainable way. He's also a founder of the first DevOpsDays Riga back in 2017, and now is leading the Latvia DevOps & Agile Community.
