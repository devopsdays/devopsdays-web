### Ignite

Thinking about contributing to or organizing a meetup? Interested in community work for internal or public communities? In this Ignite, I'll share what we've learned over the last six years of organizing the DevOps Meetup in Bern - what worked and what did not work, how to build and grow a commnity, and a few tips on how to get started.
