### Software Developer @ SorintLab

Ivan is an Italian guy who loves coding and computer stuff. He's based in a small town in the north of Italy. He works as a remote software developer for Sorint Lab. Two of his major concerns about software development are code security and tests. During his spare time, he loves sharing his knowledge with the community by giving talks and writing technical blog posts. He's also a mentor for junior developers and also for students that aim to be professional software developers in their life.
