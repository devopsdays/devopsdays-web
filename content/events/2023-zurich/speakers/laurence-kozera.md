### Psychological Safety Expert
Laurence Kozera is a consultant in psychological safety. With over a decade of experience, including a background in languages and a tenure as a former employee of Google, she has a unique ability to build trust and foster collaboration within engineering teams and partner ecosystems. Her expertise in navigating communication nuances allows for smooth understanding and cooperation, streamlining the solution-building process. Laurence excels in working with ambitious and impactful ideas driven by diverse stakeholders.
