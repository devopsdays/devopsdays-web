### Head of Quality Engineering & Senior Consultant @ SwissQ

Manuel has been part of SwissQ Consulting AG since 2022 as the specialist head of Quality Engineering. Before his time in Switzerland, the studied actor had already been working in the field of test automation and quality assurance with a focus on reviews in Vienna, Austria, for over 5 years.
