### Coding Architect & Software Engineer @ avega IT AG
Matthias Fritschi works as a Coding Architect & Software Engineer at avega IT AG, where he supports customers in successfully realizing software and continuously delivering value to the customer. His enthusiasm is not only dedicated to complex software projects but also to continuous delivery, DevOps, cloud topics and infrastructure automation. Matthias is one of the founders and organizers of the DevOps Meetup in Bern.
