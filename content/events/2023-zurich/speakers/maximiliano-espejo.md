### Betreat - Co-Founder
Maximiliano is a social entrepreneur with over a decade of working experience in the hospitality and MICE industry in Europe, USA, Middle East and Australia. Currently, he is the co-founder of Betreat a Swiss Corporate Event Agency that organize retreats, conferences and incentives based on sustainability and wellbeing.
