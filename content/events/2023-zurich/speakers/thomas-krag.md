### Genetically Modified Viking
Thomas was initially set out on a destiny of studying and becoming a professional musician. However, a year abroad changed his life and he fell in love with the IT industry. Ever since he's been in roles such as help desk, sysadmin, systems engineer, tech lead, engineering manager and product owner.

He has spoken at multiple meetups and conferences on topics such as DevOps and leadership.
