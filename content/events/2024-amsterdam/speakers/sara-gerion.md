Sara has over 12 years of experience in the tech industry, specializing in cloud development and DevOps practices. Her career has included roles in small businesses, scale-ups, and major technology corporations. In 2016, she began volunteering as the co-founder and director of technology at SheSharp, a non-profit foundation dedicated to aiding women and non-binary people in starting, sustaining, and advancing their careers in the tech industry.
