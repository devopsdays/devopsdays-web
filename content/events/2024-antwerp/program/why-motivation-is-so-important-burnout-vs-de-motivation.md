The talk delves into the critical role of motivation and life sense in personal and professional growth.
During the talk:
- We will discuss why relation between the company and employee is a partnership.
- See what are the key factors of the motivation(it's not about money)
- How people can lose and get motivation and how it contrasts the energizing effects of inspiration and involvement with the detrimental impacts of burnout and de-motivation.
