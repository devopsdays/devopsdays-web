As a co-founder of meshcloud, Christina is a multi-cloud expert. With a cloud foundation platform, meshcloud helps large organizations implement a sustainable cloud strategy and provide secure cloud environments to their IT teams. Christina runs the departments of customer success and growth at meshcloud and helps to drive cloud transformation in some of the largest German enterprises.
Before founding meshcloud, Christina worked at the German Research Center for Artificial Intelligence in Berlin in the field of Smart Data. She studied Computer Science and Business at the Technical University of Darmstadt, Germany as well as the Technical University of Berlin with stays in Belgium and France.
