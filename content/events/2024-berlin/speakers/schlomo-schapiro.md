Schlomo Schapiro is an Agile IT and Open Source enthusiast dedicated to advancing an agile mindset and a [DevOps-orientated culture](https://schlomo.schapiro.org/p/5-devops-principles.html) in IT. He works as Associate Partner / Principal Engineer at [Tektit Consulting](https://tektitconsulting.com/) in Berlin, is author of several Open Source projects, conference [speaker](https://schlomo.schapiro.org/p/publications.html) and regularly publishes [blog](https://schlomo.schapiro.org/) and [magazine articles](https://schlomo.schapiro.org/p/publications.html).
