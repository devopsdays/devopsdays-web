On March 8th 2023, Datadog had a massive global outage. It took more than 500
engineers split amongst many teams over two days to coordinate the incident
response. In this talk, I will go over the trigger of the incident and why it
took such large-scale efforts to resolve, and some of the technical and social
lessons learned from the event.
