AWS cost optimization can be a complex undertaking. In this talk, I will share
best practices and real-world examples of how organizations have saved
significant money by optimizing their AWS usage. From resource tagging to
effective scaling strategies, attendees will learn practical tips to optimize
their AWS spend.
