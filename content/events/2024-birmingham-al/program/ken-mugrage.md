Title = "Making strategic technology choices in a fast moving world"
Type = "talk"

Organizations have always struggled to keep up with the rapid pace of
technological change, let alone make effective strategic investments in the
future. Even when decisions are made, communicating and aligning them with the
rest of the organization is just as difficult.

In this talk, Ken will show the frameworks, tools, and processes Thoughtworks
has used over the past couple decades to attack this problem.
