Demethria Ramseur is a PMP-certified project manager and is a Certified Scrum
Master. She has over 20 years in managing and driving a variety of critical,
complex IT projects through to completion. Demethria is a site reliability
engineering Manager at Red Hat where she manages a team of engineers that
develop cloud-native technologies and provide operational support for the
cloud-native platform. Demethria earned her Ph.D and M.S. in Computer Science
and Software Engineering from Auburn University, and a B.S. in Computer Science
from Jackson State University.
