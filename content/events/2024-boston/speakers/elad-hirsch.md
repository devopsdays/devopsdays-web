Elad is a Tech Lead at TeraSky CTO Office, a global provider of multi-cloud, cloud-native, and innovative IT solutions. With an experience in principal engineering positions at Agmatix, JFrog, IDI, and Finjan Security, his primary areas of expertise revolve around software architecture and DevOps practices. He is a proactive advocate for fostering a DevOps culture, enabling organizations to improve their software architecture and streamline operations in cloud-native environments.
