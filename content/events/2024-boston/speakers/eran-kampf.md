Eran Kampf is the VP of Engineering for Twingate, a zero trust company that loves geeking out on DevOps, K8s, and security. Prior to Twingate, Eran worked for and consulted for a number of startups including Wix and SimilarWeb, and he co-founded Monday.com, the well-known task management platform. He also created the first location-based social network for iPhone, a foodie app called Fiddme. Eran is a native of Israel and loves everything food as much as he loves coding.
