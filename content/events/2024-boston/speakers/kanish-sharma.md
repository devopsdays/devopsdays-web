Kanish Sharma is head of product marketing at Tidelft where he oversees go-to-market strategy, messaging, positioning, and content for Tidelift's products and services working closely with product, sales, and engineering teams. Prior to joining Tidelift, Kanish was a director of product marketing at Camunda and Silk. Kanish holds a Global MBA from Suffolk University and a BS in Industrial and Systems Engineering from Rutgers University.
