Sabrina is currently Sr. Manager of a tech recruitment team at Huxley in Boston, a well-known firm working with over 50 tech teams locally and thousands of candidates. Formerly co-founding the US community of Women in DevOps, her community building work has informed her recruitment practice around the importance of building relationships and networking in the DevOps space.
