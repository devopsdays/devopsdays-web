Sara has over 13 years of experience in the tech industry, specializing in cloud development and DevOps practices. Her career has progressed from contributing to the growth of a small business and the rapid expansion of a scale-up, to holding a strategic role in a globally recognized technology corporation. Since 2016, she has also volunteered as the co-founder and director of technology at SheSharp, a non-profit foundation dedicated to helping women and non-binary people start, sustain, and advance their careers in the tech industry.
