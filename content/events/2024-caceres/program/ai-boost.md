<p>The talk covers the history of SRE, from its origins at Google to its industry-wide adoption, including Meta's Production Engineering approach. Rui discusses key trends shaping the field, including cloud-native technologies, AI integration, edge computing, security practices, and sustainability. The presentation explores challenges facing SREs, such as the skills gap and evolving security landscape, while highlighting opportunities for innovation and growth. Rui provides practical strategies for career advancement in SRE, emphasizing continuous learning, community engagement, personal branding, and embracing challenges. The talk concludes with insights into the positive job outlook for SREs and the importance of adaptability in shaping technology's future.</p>
