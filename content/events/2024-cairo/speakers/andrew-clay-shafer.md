Andrew Shafer is referenced as a foundational voice in the DevOps movement. He evangelized DevOps tools and practices before DevOps was a word. 

Andrew has been a Global Core Organizer of Devopsdays since 2010.  

He is the founder Ergonautic and was previously Vice President Transformation at Red Hat.
