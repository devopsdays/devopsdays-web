Helen Beal is CEO & Chair of Value Stream Management Consortium; and the Head of Ambassador Program of PeopleCert for DevOps Institute.  

Helen is an award-winning DevOps speaker, writer, and strategic advisor. She is chair of the DevNetwork DevOps Advisory Board and provides strategic advisory services to DevOps and VSM industry leaders.
