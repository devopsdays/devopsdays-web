Raj Iyer is the Chief Information Officer for North America at GM Financial, where he spearheads the strategic direction of IT initiatives. With a robust background in financial services technology, Raj has held key leadership positions at Silicon Valley Bank, JPMorgan Chase, Capital One, Deloitte, and IBM. His expertise spans digital transformation, IT strategy, and building large-scale platforms to support business growth. Raj is dedicated to improving customer experience and scaling business functions, making him a pivotal leader in technological innovation and operational excellence.
