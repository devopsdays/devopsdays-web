Amanda is CEO of Hunted Labs from Long Beach, California - a software supply chain security company. She was previously Head of Operations of a small business and worked on advertising campaigns for various clients as part of an Latinx advertising agency and Mazda Motor Corporations.

As a Latina leader, she strives to achieve her goals with kindness and perseverance. She enjoys fitness, going to the beach, and movie nights.
