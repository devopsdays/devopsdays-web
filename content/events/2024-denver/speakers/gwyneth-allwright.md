Gwyneth is a senior software engineer at Invoca, where she works on site reliability engineering. Her previous experience includes technical roles on developer enablement and data science teams. She grew up in South Africa and initially embarked on an academic career path before pivoting into technology. She obtained a Master's degree in physics from the University of Waterloo, Ontario and a Master's degree in computer science from the University of California, Santa Barbara. Gwyneth is passionate about reducing tech debt and making infrastructure upgrades more manageable (perhaps even fun) for everyone involved.
