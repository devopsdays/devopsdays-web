Sylvain Garabiol, Agile coach for the last 10 years, married, two daughters, concerned about the biggest transition that we are going through, still trying to figure out how to participate in it in a positive way.

1 in 10 billion, stuck on a rocky planetary body, going through the immensity of space wondering what will come of it, will elegance or brutality be the biggest outcome? Nobody will remain to be the judge of it, and still I think we should care!
