Meet [Frank Boucher](https://www.linkedin.com/in/fboucheros/), a seasoned IT professional with nearly two decades of experience in the industry. Specializing in Microsoft Azure, Frank is a trusted advisor in major Canadian markets like Ottawa and Montreal, as well as internationally (Minsk).

Frank's expertise has earned him prestigious accolades, including four-time Microsoft Azure MVP and Microsoft Azure Advisor status. In his quest to share knowledge, Frank launched the "Cloud 5 minutes" show, delivering bite-sized technical insights in both English and French.

As an influential figure in the Microsoft Azure community, Frank contributes to the field through book reviews and maintains active blogs. He has also contributed to notable open-source projects such as Azure User Management Console (AUMC) and CoreWiki.
