Organizations should kickstart platform engineering by defining their vision for platform engineering, understanding unique platform team composition needs and clarifying the skills and behaviors of effective platform team members. This session will showcase Gartner's benchmarking on high-value platform engineering team skills and compositions and present case study examples of organizations who have done this well.
