Platform Teams are all the rage these days.  But how do they relate to DevOps?  Is it something brand new?  Is it an evolution?  How would I even go about starting a Platform Team or knowing if I already have one under a different title?  In this talk, Eric will demystify the fabled Platform Team, including how it relates to DevOps as a whole and some pragmatic advice on kicking off or maturing your own efforts!
