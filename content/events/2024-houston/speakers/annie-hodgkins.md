Annie Hodgkins (Garcia) works as a Director, Analyst supporting Software Engineering Leaders at Gartner. She provides actionable advice and recommendations to  engineering leaders across industries in topic areas including software engineering strategy and governance, platform engineering, organizational design and change management, agile development and methodologies and hiring, developing and attracting engineering talent. Her coverage also includes citizen development strategies and governance, fusion teams, API strategy, application rationalization and technical debt management.
