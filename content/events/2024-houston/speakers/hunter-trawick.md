Hunter Trawick is a software engineer and team lead with a passion for creating enterprise-grade software. With experience in both failed and successful startups, he currently focuses on optimizing RentRedi, a leading solution for small to medium-sized landlords. Hunter has a primary skill set in full stack JavaScript with React and Angular, Firebase and Google Cloud. He has contributed to projects including insurance, social media, human resources, event seating, chat w/ translation, fitness, and landlord topics. He is also pursuing a Master's degree in Management from the University of Illinois.
