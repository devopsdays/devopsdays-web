Continuous Deployment is all about increasing efficiency and reducing errors in a collaborative environment. But there are common antipatterns that can trip you up and squash your team’s productivity. Whether you’re starting to explore continuous deployment or preparing for the next wave of enhancements, come learn how to avoid these pitfalls, and set up your CI/CD pipelines for efficiency, speed, and reliability.
