Technical Account Manager, Datadog

Ayesha Bhutto is a Technical Account Manager at Datadog, and lives in Toronto, Canada. Her career has spanned with the Support Team and as a previous Technical Account Manager for IBM. She holds a Bachelors of Engineering from McMaster University with a lot of focus on project management and technical success. When she's not working, she is probably exploring the outdoors or most likely shopping.
