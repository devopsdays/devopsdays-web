Vijay is an engineer dedicated to empowering other engineers to build faster and more easily. He has a background in programming language design and implementation, with a publication in OOPSLA and prior work at Meta on the open-source programming language Flow. He is now at Oso, a company focused on helping people solve the challenges of implementing and maintaining authorization in their applications.
