Jason Yee is a Staff Developer Advocate at Datadog where he helps engineers and engineering organizations improve by using data, collaborating, and learning. Previously, he worked at Gremlin, O’Reilly Media, and MongoDB. He’s also a DevOpsDays global co-chair and core team member. When he’s not working or organizing DevOps conferences, he likes to play with his puppy, drink whiskey, and catch pokemon.
