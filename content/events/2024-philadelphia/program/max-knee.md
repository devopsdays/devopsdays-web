At the New York Times, we've undertaken a developer experience journey many cloud providers, CI/CD tools to a developer productivity platform that contains a single target platform, separate CI and CD tools that will handle new and existing applications.

This talk will provide background on what we're doing at the Times and focus specifically on our CD platform and how we help teams adopt continuous delivery and provide a singular platform for it.
