Let's delve into the transformative potential of internal platforms in driving digital transformation within large enterprises. We will explore how the IDP can enhance developer experience, promoting agility, fostering innovation, and facilitating collaboration. Through a practical example and lessons learned from building and implementing our own IDP, I will share insights into overcoming challenges, aligning stakeholders, and maximizing the impact of internal platforms on organizational growth.
