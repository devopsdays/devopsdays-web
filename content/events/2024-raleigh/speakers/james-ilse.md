James Ilse is a Senior Solutions Engineer at Solo.io with more than 20 years of development and infrastructure work behind him. Before joining Solo.io he was working at hypergrowth companies and scaling with their demand from ten requests per second to ten thousand. In the last few years he has been enthralled with all things Kubernetes, especially the natural progression from first few clusters to a full service mesh.
