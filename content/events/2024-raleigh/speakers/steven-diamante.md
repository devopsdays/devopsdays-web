Steven Diamante is a software craftsman who's purpose is to coach development teams on technical excellence so that they can have more fun and deliver more business value. He enjoys working collaboratively in a highly agile environment where experiments and learning is heavily encouraged. Steven is the organizer of the Raleigh Software Craftsmanship Community.
