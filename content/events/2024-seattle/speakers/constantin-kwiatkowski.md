As a speaker, I bring a unique blend of hands-on experience, industry knowledge, and a passion for innovation. Whether discussing the intricacies of software engineering, the nuances of Cloud and DevOps, or the specialized realm of SAP BTP/CPI, I am dedicated to delivering engaging and informative sessions that empower and inspire.
