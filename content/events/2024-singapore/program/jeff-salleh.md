### Ignite

Keeping the lights on for a cloud service is more complicated than it seems, it is like the Elegant Swan - gliding with elegance in motion while hiding the activities going on beneath the surface. 

With running a cloud service globally, there are a few lessons that could be shared  on how do we scale out quickly dealing from the time to market, locality compliance and security, auditing.

While there are challenges, there are also means and ways that can elevate the pain and I can share the top 5 things SRE can/should consider in running the cloud operations for maintaining compliance and configurations.
