    <p>Vice President, Architecture at Fidelity Investments | Speaker | Author</p>
    <h3 class="speaker-bio-talks">Wrangling Compliance in the Wild West of Software Delivery</h3>
    <p>Wrangling Compliance in the Wild West of Software Delivery” encapsulates the dynamic struggle of ensuring adherence to regulations and standards in the ever-evolving realm of software development. Product teams and organizations supporting the product teams must navigate through shifting landscapes of technology, regulation, and best practices. This talk delves into the challenges of maintaining compliance in a fast-paced environment where innovation often outpaces regulation. From implementing robust security measures to conducting thorough audits, attendees will learn strategies for taming the compliance beast while still fostering innovation and agility. Through humorous anecdotes and practical insights, this presentation will try to offer a fresh perspective on an often daunting subject.
    </p>

  <p>We're excited to announce DevOpsDays DC 2024 on September 25-26, 2024!</p>

  <p>We'll be back at the {{< event_link page="location" text="American Red Cross Headquarters" >}} in Washington, DC. It's an amazing venue with a lot of history.</p>

  We're also excited to be teaming up again with Kubernetes Community Day DC who will be hosting their [event on September 24, 2024](https://community.cncf.io/kcd-washington-dc/)! Whether you are a developer, ops pro, system engineer, or any other IT professional excited about cloud native technologies, Kubernetes Community Days DC (KCD DC) will have something for you. Supported by the Cloud Native Computing Foundation, their 2024 event will also be hosted at the historic national headquarters of the American Red Cross. This full-day conference will focus on knowledge sharing, best practices, and new developments in and around Kubernetes.

  Follow us on [Twitter](https://twitter.com/DevOpsDaysDC) or [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/devopsdays-dc/) to get all the latest updates and announcements.
