### Ignite

Let us explore the transformative power of DevOps in dismantling traditional barriers within Enterprise Architecture. In just five minutes, we walk through the challenges of an isolated, slow-moving "ivory tower" and highlight how DevOps serves as a catalyst for collaboration, agility, and cultural change.
