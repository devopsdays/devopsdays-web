### SRE @ elastic
Daniel Aschwanden is a SRE Engineering Manager at Elastic's Cloud Capacity Team. He has enjoyed working with all system and security related things ever since laying his hands on Linux more than 20 years ago and is especially interested in building monitoring systems and automation to improve system reliability.
