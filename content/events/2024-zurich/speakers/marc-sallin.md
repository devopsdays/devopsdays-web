### Solution Architect @ Swiss Post
During his ten years in software development, Marc Sallin became convinced that high speed, quality, and low costs are not in competition but form a symbiosis. Guided by this idea, he has been shaping the future of parcel sorting at Swiss Post as a solution architect for five years.
