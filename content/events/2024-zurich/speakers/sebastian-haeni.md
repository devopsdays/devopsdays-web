### Lead Architect @ FAIRTIQ
Sebastian Häni, Lead Architect at FAIRTIQ, is a driving force behind critical initiatives at his organization aimed at enhancing engineering processes. He focuses on establishing a robust reliability framework, streamlining architecture decision-making, implementing innovative solutions, and optimizing data management. Sebastian holds an MSc degree in Engineering with specialization in Data Science.
