### Solution Architekt & DevOps Engineer @ Zürcher Kantonalbank
Since joining Zürcher Kantonalbank in 2022 as a Solution Architect and DevOps Engineer, I've immersed myself in the payment domain, leveraging my background in computer science from Vienna. My freelance experience prior to this role enriched my understanding of practical applications in technology. I have a strong foundation in distributed system technologies, Spring and cloud technologies. These skills have been crucial in my work, enabling me to design effective solutions and improve processes within our team. I'm passionate about learning and applying new technologies to solve complex problems.
