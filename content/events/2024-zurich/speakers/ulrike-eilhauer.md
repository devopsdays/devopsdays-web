### Digital Consultant @ Zühlke
Ulrike Eilhauer has been with Zühlke since June 2023. In her M.Sc. of Physics Engineering, she specialized in sustainable technologies. She has worked in the IT industry since 2015 and found her passion for enterprise architectures in 2019. She holds a TOGAF and SAFe certification and started to teach SAFe DevOps courses. Ulrike came already into contact with various industries: transportation, telecommunications, the energy sector and retail.
