In the fast-paced world of technology, where downtime equals lost revenue, DevOps practices aren't just helpful; they are essential. But what does it take to design and operate mission-critical systems that must perform under very high pressure? In this talk, I’ll share real-world lessons from my experience in building and scaling resilient IT operations in high-stakes environments.

We’ll explore how to build high-performing teams, balance velocity with quality, and automate as much as possible. We will go from overcoming cultural resistance to implementing fail-safe processes, this session will provide you with actionable insights and strategies that are immediately valuable for your own DevOps ecosystems.
