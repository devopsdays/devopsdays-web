Principal Engineer at Uber

Jeppe Brønsted is a Principal Engineer at Uber, he has helped develop and operate complex systems from container orchestration systems over scalable databases to ledger storage.
As a Principal Engineer Jeppe sets the direction for engineering culture and practices. Jeppe teaches this through living the values based on empathy, respect and deep technical knowledge.
