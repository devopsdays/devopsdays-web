CTO @ Eficode

Marko Klemetti is a tech advisor guiding organizations with the experience of a thousand others.

He is an active speaker, and you can find him not only on The DEVOPS Conference stage but as a speaker at various other conferences.

Marko is still a passionate programmer, working mostly on emerging technologies that have the potential to become mainstream in the future. That helps him gain a very in-depth understanding of the trends and new possibilities. As part of his work, Marko is also a founder and advisor in many tech startups.
