Senior Cloud Solution Architect, Microsoft

Rasmus Lystrøm, M.Sc. is a Senior Cloud Solution Architect at Microsoft with a diverse background serving as a company commander and battalion chief of staff in the army, lecturing on software development as an associate professor at the IT University of Copenhagen, developing airport management systems as a lead software developer, consulting all kinds Microsoft, DevOps, and Azure as a Principal Consult in Microsoft Services and currently disrupting all things cloud and DevOps while speaking about it publicly.
