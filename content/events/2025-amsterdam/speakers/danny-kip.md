Danny Kip has been a DevOps Engineer for 27 years. Yes, that pre-dates the term.
Working as an ICT contractor around the country, he has seen all the mistakes people and organisations make, and will repeat them for new organisations everywhere. The directors of his company are nudging him towards teaching people to make less mistakes, probably just to get his hands off the keyboard. So here we are.
