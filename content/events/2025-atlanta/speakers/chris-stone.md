Chris is a software engineer in Atlanta with a passion for sharing knowledge
with others. He has experience in various languages, including TypeScript, Go,
C#, and PHP, and enjoys writing simple, readable, and accessible code. He is a
proponent of mob programming, test-driven development, regular refactoring, and
safely managing technical debt. He is involved with the Software Crafters
Atlanta meetup and he works as a Senior Software Engineer at Flashlight
Learning.
