Joan Esquivel is a seasoned quality assurance engineer with over 7 years of
software testing and automation expertise. Specializing in integrating
Domain-Driven Design principles into QA practices, Joan has driven impactful
testing strategies that align technical execution with business objectives.

An advocate for knowledge-sharing, Joan creates tutorials and resources for a
growing YouTube community and frequently speaks at conferences and workshops.
His innovative approach to combining QA management with software architecture
makes him a trusted voice in the industry, committed to helping teams deliver
high-quality software efficiently.
