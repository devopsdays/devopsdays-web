From film to engineering, Annie’s career has seen many iterations. She is a keen observer of what works and what doesn’t, striving always to make the right thing to do the easy thing to do. Annie and her husband, Michael, are raising their three teenagers in Boulder, CO. She’s currently working on a project to make doing the right thing with team management the easy thing to do.
