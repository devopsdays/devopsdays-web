How can we spend so much money in cybersecurity and still get pwned ? How password policies / MFA became users nightmare ? Maybe it's where you ain't looking... Let's dive into a real world problem that is Security Fatigue, how users be/let hackers arm your precious company.
Let's raise our global awareness about security and good practices that will make your users not bored af when thinking about cybersecurity.
We will see some use cases based on our experience and (try) to make it fun.
