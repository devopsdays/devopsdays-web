Rafael Vasquez is a software developer on the Open Technology team at IBM. He previously completed an MASc. focused on self-driving car research and transitioned from a data scientist role in retail to his current role, where he continues to develop his passion for open-source and AI model serving and deployment.

Most recently, he has been dedicated to open-source projects such as vLLM and KServe ModelMesh.
