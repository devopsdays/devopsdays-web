Carl is a software architect at Oracle. Most of his career has been focused on evolving and scaling the service infrastructure for their core electronic medical record platform called Millennium. He is now working with on their next-generation EHR within Oracle Health. He is passionate about growing a positive engineering culture, optimizing how software teams operate, and working on interesting problems in healthcare. In his spare time, he enjoys blogging about engineering related topics and sharing his poorly made illustrations at https://che55er.io.
