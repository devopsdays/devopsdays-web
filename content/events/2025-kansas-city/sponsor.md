We greatly value sponsors for this open event. If you are interested in sponsoring, please drop us an email at {{< email_organizers subject="Sponsor - DevOpsDays KC 2025">}}. We are always open to discussing custom packages for sponsorships or collaboration, and with us <span style="color: red; font-size: 1.10rem; font-weight: 900;">co-locating with KCDC for 2025</span> there are some opportunites to reach a wider audience.

<h3><a href="https://drive.google.com/file/d/1Yb8JkH0wRZBL2op3QCLvF-Ua6lhmEFRe/view?usp=drive_link" target="_blank">2025 Sponsorship Prospectus available</a> - sponsor both events and get a 10% discount! Contact us for more information.</h3>

<hr>

DevOpsDays KC is a self-organizing conference for practitioners that depends on sponsorships. We do not have vendor booths, sell product presentations, or distribute attendee contact lists. Sponsors have the opportunity to have short elevator pitches during the program and will get recognition on the website and social media before, during, and after the event. Sponsors are encouraged to represent themselves by actively participating and engaging with the attendees as peers. Any attendee also has the opportunity to demo products/projects as part of an open space session.
<p>
Each sponsor receives a table where they can interact with those interested to come visit during breaks, with <span style="font-weight: bold;">Gold sponsors</span> being placed in a prominent, high-profile location (first come, first served). All attendees are welcome to propose any subject they want during the <a href="https://devopsdays.org/open-space-format/" target="_blank">open spaces</a>, but this is a community-focused conference, so heavy marketing will probably work against you when trying to make a good impression on the attendees.
<p>
The best thing to do is send engineers to interact with the experts at DevOpsDays on their own terms.
<p>
