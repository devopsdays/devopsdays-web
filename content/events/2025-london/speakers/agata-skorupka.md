Agata Skorupka is a Senior Cloud DevOps Engineer and Data Scientist with a passion for building resilient cloud platforms and bridging the gap between infrastructure and data. She holds a PhD in Data Science and shares her expertise as an academic lecturer, bringing a strong research-driven foundation to her practical work.

Agata has hands-on experience designing secure multi-tenant architectures, scaling platform engineering initiatives, and applying data-driven methods to improve operational practices. Her unique blend of academic rigor and real-world engineering allows her to tackle complex challenges across cloud environments, platform development, and operational resilience.
