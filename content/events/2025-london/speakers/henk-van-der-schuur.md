Henk van der Schuur is Customer Director at Schuberg Philis, a company without managers, operating with customer-dedicated expert teams. Schuberg Philis delivers high-quality, mission-critical solutions with end-to-end responsibility in vital sectors.

Henk is a passionate pioneer with years of experience in product management (AFAS Software), IT strategy, and business development. He holds a PhD in Information Science (Utrecht University, 2011).
