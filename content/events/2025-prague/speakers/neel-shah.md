Neel Shah is a DevOps engineer and community leader with over 3 years of experience in the tech industry. He is passionate about building and maintaining scalable and reliable systems. He is also a strong advocate for open source software and has contributed to several projects.

Currently ,Neel Shah is leading the CNCF, HashiCorp, and Docker Ahmedabad and Gandhinagar chapters. He has also mentored more than 15+ hackathons and has helped many aspiring engineers get started in their careers.
