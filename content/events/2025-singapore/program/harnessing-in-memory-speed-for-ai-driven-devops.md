### Ignite

As DevOps teams increasingly rely on AI to drive automation and proactive decision-making, real‑time data becomes the backbone of innovation. In this session, discover how Redis Enterprise’s in‑memory capabilities deliver the low latency and high throughput needed for AI-enhanced DevOps practices—from rapid data ingestion and caching to powering predictive operations. Learn how to integrate Redis Enterprise into your toolchain and transform operations in the age of generative AI.
