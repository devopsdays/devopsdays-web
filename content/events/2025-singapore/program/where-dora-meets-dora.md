### Ignite

DevOps DORA Metrics: Key performance metrics (Deployment Frequency, Lead Time for Changes, Change Failure Rate, Mean Time to Recovery) and their role in continuous improvement. - Digital Operational Resilience Act: An overview of regulatory requirements for cybersecurity, incident reporting, business continuity, and managing third-party risks. - Benefits: How integrating these frameworks leads to better performance, enhanced security, regulatory compliance, and improved risk management.

Participants will learn how to align their DevOps practices with the Digital Operational Resilience Act, ensuring their teams are both high-performing and secure.
