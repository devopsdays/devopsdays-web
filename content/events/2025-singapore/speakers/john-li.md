John Li is the Head of Client Technologies at Endowus, having spent nearly a decade building consumer-focused financial services. He is an inspirational and hands-on engineering leader who combines his strong product sense and technological skills to ensure that products and solutions are purpose-built to meet specific needs. His keen understanding of data analysis and marketing technology helps to guide crucial product strategies and decisions.

Prior to joining Endowus, John worked at Goldman Sachs and StashAway, developing equity research and asset management solutions.
