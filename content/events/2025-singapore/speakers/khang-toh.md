As Head of Technology for SRE and Cloud Infrastructure at Bank Jago, Khang drives the strategic design, delivery, and evolution of a resilient, high-performance cloud ecosystem powering seamless digital banking for over 16 million customers. He leads cross-functional efforts to embed Site Reliability Engineering (SRE) principles, developer experience, and scalable cloud operations as core enablers of business growth and agility. Under his leadership, the bank has strengthened its operational excellence, service reliability, and developer productivity, aligning technology execution with bold business objectives.

With over 20 years of experience spanning software development, system architecture, and large-scale cloud infrastructure, Khang has consistently delivered complex, mission-critical systems with a clear focus on reliability, scalability, and business impact. As Conference Chair for droidcon SG, he champions industry collaboration and continuous learning, reflecting a deep commitment to growing the regional tech ecosystem.
