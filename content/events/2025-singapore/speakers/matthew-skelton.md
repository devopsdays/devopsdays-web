Matthew Skelton is one of the foremost leaders in modern organizational dynamics for fast flow, drawing on Team Topologies, Adapt Together™️, and related practices to support organizations with transformation towards a sustainable fast flow of value and true business agility via holistic innovation.

Co-author of the award-winning and ground-breaking book Team Topologies, Founder and CEO at Conflux, and director of core operations at the non-profit Team Topologies, Matthew brings a humane approach to organizational effectiveness.
