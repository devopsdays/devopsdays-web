Seasoned technology leader with over 25 years of experience in driving strategic solution design and implementation to elevate customer experience through resilient and reliable systems. He currently serves as the Director of the Site Reliability Engineering (SRE) Centre of Enablement at Barclays.

Prior to joining Barclays in 2025, Rajiv spent his entire career at Citibank, where he played a pivotal role in strengthening service management capabilities across the Consumer and Wealth business. He led the adoption of SRE practices, significantly enhancing technology resilience and operational efficiency. Rajiv is deeply passionate about service management, developing future-ready capabilities, and fostering leadership while championing cultural transformation with a strong focus on end-user needs.
