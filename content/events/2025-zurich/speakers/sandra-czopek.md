### Product Owner @From Poland With Dev
The author is passionate about effective leadership and communication. She has a master's degree in Project Management (SGH Warsaw School of Economics) and a degree in Computer Science (AGH University of Science and Technology in Krakow). She has experience working as a Project Manager in an international environment, managing projects for startup and corporate companies all over the world. Currently, she is working as a Product Owner. She actively collaborates with the team and stakeholders, on each product phase, to ensure that the product has the greatest possible value.
