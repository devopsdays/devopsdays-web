Innovation isn't about getting it right. It's about getting it less wrong, over
and over, until something works.

Ajuna Kyaruzi will share about the failures that shaped her career journey. The
ones she hid from, the ones she learned from, and why the shame of falling
behind was always more dangerous than the failure itself. You'll leave with
skills that turn "fail fast" from a platitude into a practice: empathy,
community, and perseverance.

You're building and learning quickly in this new world of agentic workflows.
Some of it won't work. That's okay, it's better that you tried.
