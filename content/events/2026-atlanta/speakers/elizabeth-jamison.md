Elizabeth Jamison is a software engineer, perpetual student, and advocate of
community in the tech space. After pivoting from implementation consulting into
software engineering, she worked on Payments at Square. Recently she is taking
time off from working to pursue her Master's in Computer Science at Georgia
Tech and explore various nerdy interests such as internet routing. Because the
Atlanta tech community was so important in her career pivot to software
engineering, she tries to pay it forward where possible and help facilitate and
participate in community events where possible.
