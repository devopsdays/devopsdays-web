You will mostly find me building and innovating in the startup ecosystem. I
founded two startups, Housepital and AlphaEd, and my journey has been driven by
a passion for building solutions that tackle real-world challenges. I am a
member of Google Women Techmakers, Google for Startups Women Founder, and an
active member in various tech communities; I enjoy collaborating and leading.

I have built a strong technical background in full-stack development, mainly
with the MERN stack, and developed multiple full-stack applications. At
EdVenture Park, I’ve helped over 100+ startups as a student entreprenuer with
market validation and growth strategies. I've also been a speaker and
roundtable panelist at various events, including discussions hosted by the
British High Commissioner, where I shared insights on tech innovation and
entrepreneurship.

Whether it's contributing to open-source, building Next.js applications, or
engaging in thought leadership, I’m always exploring new ways to solve complex
challenges and give back to the community.
