Jeff Diecks is a Technical Project Manager at The Linux Foundation leading
OpenSSF’s support of the AIxCC program.

Jeff has more than two decades of experience in technology and communications
with a diverse background in operations, project management and executive
leadership. A participant in open source since 1999, he’s delivered digital
products and applications for universities, sports leagues, state governments,
global media companies and non-profits.
