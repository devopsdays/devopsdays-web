Omari Gaskins Jr. is the creator of DocuSOR, the first platform that executes
and validates documentation to prevent drift and accelerate developer
onboarding. As a Software Engineer at JPMorgan Chase for the last 5 years, he
has worked on hundreds of different applications, with on prem and public cloud
integrations, managing the scale that comes with working for a large global
enterprise.
