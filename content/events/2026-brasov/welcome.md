# Welcome to devopsdays Brașov

Welcome to **devopsdays Brașov**, a community-driven conference focused on the intersection of **development, operations, and organizational culture**.

devopsdays Brașov is part of the global **devopsdays** series — non-profit events organized by local communities, for the benefit of the wider tech ecosystem. Our goal is to create a space where practitioners can share real-world experiences, learn from one another, and have honest conversations about how we build and run modern systems.

---

## What is devopsdays?

devopsdays is a worldwide series of technical conferences that emphasize:
- **Experience reports** over vendor pitches
- **Learning through discussion**, not just presentations
- **Open Spaces**, where participants set the agenda
- A balance of **technical and human topics**

Common themes include (but are not limited to):
- DevOps, SRE, and Platform Engineering
- Cloud, Kubernetes, and Infrastructure as Code
- Observability, reliability, and security
- Team dynamics, organizational change, and leadership

---

## Why Brașov?

Brașov is a growing technology hub and a natural meeting point for diverse engineering communities. Surrounded by mountains and rich in history, it offers an excellent environment for focused discussions, collaboration, and meaningful connections.

devopsdays Brașov aims to be:
- **Community-first**
- **Inclusive and welcoming**
- **Practical and experience-driven**
- **Free of sales and marketing bias**

---

## Code of Conduct

All participants, speakers, sponsors, and organizers are expected to follow the **devopsdays Code of Conduct**. We are committed to providing a safe, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

---

## Get involved

devopsdays Brașov is built by the community. You can get involved by:
- Submitting a proposal during the **Call for Paper**
- Participating in or facilitating **Open Spaces**
- Volunteering or providing feedback
- Supporting the event as a **sponsor**

---

## See you at devopsdays Brașov

Whether you are new to DevOps or have years of hands-on experience, devopsdays Brașov is a place to learn, share, and connect with others facing similar challenges.

Welcome to the community.
