## DevOpsDays Kraków 2026

Join us for the next edition of DevOpsDays Kraków, a renowned event, in Kraków on July 4th at ECHO Miasta Kraków. Experience insightful lectures on the latest developments in the DevOps field and network with like-minded individuals.

## What is DevOpsDays Kraków?

DevOpsDays Kraków is a part of the international DevOpsDays series, designed for developers, IT operations, management, CEO's, security, and sys admins. The conference covers topics of all DevOps pillars: organizational culture and cultural shift, lean processes, automation, monitoring, shared knowledge and responsibility, and security. If you're into DevOps practices and culture, this conference is for you!

## Why is it worth to join us?

Our participants come from various backgrounds, including software development, system administration, QA, product and engineering management, business analytics, and security. This diverse group of practitioners will share their knowledge and experiences on delivering technology projects with the latest trends. Come and join us to learn and share the latest IT solutions and cultural experiences in the tech community.
