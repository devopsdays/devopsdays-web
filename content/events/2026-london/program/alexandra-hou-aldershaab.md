My move into DevOps was a direct response to the prejudice I faced as a developer. I needed a space where the quality of my code was the only thing that mattered, and I discovered that a robust pipeline is more than a tool—it's an instrument for fairness. I'll share my journey and the strategies I used to engineer bias out of the development process.
