Supplier contract ending soon? Limited budget? Tight deadline? A thousand or so opinionated users? 4 years of data spaghetti to migrate? Follow this simple guide to Launch Success!


In September 2025, MHCLG launched Share Homes for Ukraine Data ('Share'), a system for secure data sharing between central government, local authorities and devolved administrations. Share replaced a proprietary system managed by a third party supplier. We hoped to reduce our running costs, have full control over the data and code, and to expand to future use cases. It turned out the launch was anything but simple, but one year on, boiling it down into a 30 minute conference talk seems like a good alternative to therapy.
