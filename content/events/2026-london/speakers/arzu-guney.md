Arzu is a software developer focused on building practical web and mobile applications, with interests in artificial intelligence, accessibility, and user-centred product development. She is also deeply involved in AI projects, particularly in natural language processing (NLP), machine learning, and prompt engineering.

She is the creator of Dottinoo, an education-focused side project for UK classrooms that explores personalised and accessible learning tasks for learners who need additional support. Through building Dottinoo and preparing it for external review, Arzu became especially interested in the gap between working software and trustworthy software, including production readiness, accessibility, documentation, feedback loops, and the confidence needed for others to evaluate and use a system.

Arzu is also active in the tech community through Women Coding Community and Lonca, and creates educational content to make coding and technology more approachable for learners and early-career developers.
