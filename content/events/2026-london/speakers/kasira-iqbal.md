Head of Delivery at Cogworks
Kasira is an experienced Head of Delivery and Project Manager passionate about helping teams deliver digital products that meet client needs without compromising on quality. She enjoys working closely with clients, developers, and stakeholders to keep projects on track while finding practical ways to work more efficiently and build sustainable, long-lasting solutions.

Having spoken at local meetups, Kasira enjoys sharing insights into delivery, collaboration, and creating environments where teams can thrive. She aims to share how we can be more efficient and innovative with problem-solving to produce more affordable, sustainable, and performant web applications for clients, ultimately reducing costs and saving energy.
