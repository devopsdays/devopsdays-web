Laurence Pakenham-Smith is Director of Site Reliability Engineering at Kevel, where she leads platform reliability, security, compliance and governance. With over a decade of engineering experience spanning the NHS, GDS, consulting, and high-growth SaaS, she's spent her career asking uncomfortable questions about why infrastructure is so hard to reason about, and how we can use technology from the 1960s to fix it!
