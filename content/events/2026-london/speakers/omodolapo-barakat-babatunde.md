Omodolapo Babatunde is a cloud engineer, product leader, and community builder focused on inclusive digital innovation. She has led design and growth initiatives and supports community-driven architecture programmes across Europe, North America, and Africa.

An Associate Member of the British Computer Society (AMBCS) and recognised as one of Enginuity’s 365 Faces of STEM 2025.

As a builder, speaker, and founder, she is passionate about making technology more accessible, resilient, and people-focused through both technical practice and community impact initiatives.
