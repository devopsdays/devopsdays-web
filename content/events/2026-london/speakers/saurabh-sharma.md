Saurabh is a Platform and DevOps Engineer currently at Goldman Sachs, where he works on multi-account observability and cloud infrastructure at scale. Before that, he was the sole platform engineer at Protectonce, a cybersecurity startup, where he built and owned the entire AWS infrastructure from the ground up i.e. CI/CD pipelines, serverless architecture, and deployment tooling, just after completing bachelors.
He learned platform engineering the way most people actually do: by making decisions nobody assigned him, shipping things that worked, and figuring out why later.
