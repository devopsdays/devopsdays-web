Martin Reynolds, Field CTO at Harness, is a technology leader with over 30 years of experience shaping how organizations build, deliver, and operate software at scale. He specializes in DevOps, DevSecOps, Platform Engineering, and Developer Experience (DevEx), with a growing focus on AI and intelligent automation in software delivery.

A trusted voice in the industry, Martin’s insights have appeared in leading publications such as The New Stack, LeadDev, and other prominent technology outlets. He translates complex trends—such as platform engineering, AI agents in software lifecycles, and continuous delivery at scale—into practical strategies that engineering teams can use right away.

Before joining Harness, Martin led large-scale cloud transformation initiatives. He established and scaled DevOps practices across enterprise environments. Today, he works closely with engineering leaders. He helps them improve developer productivity, boost system resilience, and optimize cost and governance in complex delivery ecosystems.
Martin brings a practitioner’s perspective to topics like internal developer platforms, software delivery automation, and the evolution toward AI-augmented engineering. He helps organizations move beyond tooling. He focuses on building high-performing, developer-centric platforms that enable faster, safer, and more efficient innovation.
