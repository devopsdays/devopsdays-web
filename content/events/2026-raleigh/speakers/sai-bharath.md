Sai Bharath is a Senior Cloud Infrastructure Engineer specializing in DevOps, SRE, automation, and large-scale observability engineering. He builds resilient cloud-native platforms across Azure and Kubernetes, with a focus on proactive outage detection, distributed systems reliability, 
and multi-signal monitoring. Sai has led automation initiatives, platform reliability programs, and cloud-scale diagnostics pipelines for enterprise environments supporting mission-critical workloads. 
Sai is passionate about bridging the gap between development and operations by designing systems that detect failures early, self-heal gracefully, and empower engineering teams with actionable insights.
