## How to get to Ramada Hotel Sibiu


### From Sibiu Railway Station

- **On foot:** around **17 minutes** (approx. **1.3 km**)
- **By taxi / rideshare:** a short ride, usually the fastest option
- **By public transport:** several bus lines stop near the hotel area, including **1, 5, 11, 13, and 22**
- **Nearest stops to the hotel:** **Casa Armatei**, **A. Șaguna**, **Zidul Cetății**

### From Sibiu International Airport

- **By taxi:** around **8 minutes**
- **By public transport:** take **Tursib line 11** from the airport toward the city; get off near **Casa Armatei / A. Șaguna**
- **Bus travel time:** around **13 minutes**, or about **17 minutes total** including the short walk to the hotel
- **Ticket:** a **60-minute urban ticket costs 3.5 lei**
- **On foot:** around **1 hour 7 minutes** (approx. **5.6 km**)

### Useful note

The airport has a **Tursib bus stop directly in front of the terminal**, so public transport is a practical option for reaching the venue.
