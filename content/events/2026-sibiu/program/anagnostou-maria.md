NIS2 and the Cyber Resilience Act (CRA) are reshaping DevOps by mandating security by design and Secure Development Lifecycle (SDLC). This presentation will take you through the biggest changes companies need to make in their SDLC, and how they relate to standards as ISO 27001, EN 303 645 and IEC 62443.
