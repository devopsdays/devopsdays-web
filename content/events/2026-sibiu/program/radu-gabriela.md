SPOFs (single point of failure) are no longer reduced to harware or software but are part of the very structure of your IAM policies. From local stale users to overpriviledged human admins with multiple identities across your system, key points of failure are everywhere, in cloud and on prem.
DevSecOps and Zero trust principles work together to find and disperse these nexus points and together we'll explore 3 key examples of how this is done. SPOFs in identity management should be a thing of the past after this session.
