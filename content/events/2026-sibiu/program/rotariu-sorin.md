In an industry where everything changes fast, “DevOps: Past, Present and Future” offers a clear look at how DevOps has evolved from a cultural movement into a strategic pillar of digital transformation. The session will explore key lessons from the past, today’s challenges for IT teams, and the impact of AI, automation, security, and platform engineering on the future of DevOps.
What participants will take away: a practical perspective on where DevOps is heading, ideas they can apply in their own organizations, and a clearer understanding of how DevOps helps build more agile, secure, and competitive companies.
