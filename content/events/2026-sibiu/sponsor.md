<h1>Hello World!</h1>

<p>
  First off, a huge thank you for your interest in supporting <strong>DevOpsDays Sibiu</strong>. 
  Our community thrives because of partners who recognize the value of sharing knowledge, 
  fostering local talent, and, of course, having a little bit of fun along the way.
</p>

<p>
  We’ve designed our sponsorship packages to offer flexibility, whether you want to give back 
  to the tech ecosystem, boost your brand’s visibility, or recruit top-tier talent.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>Community &amp; Education (Bronze or Community Sponsors)</h2>
<p><strong>Invest in the people who make our industry move forward.</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Sponsor a Speaker:</strong> Help us bring world-class expertise to the Sibiu stage.</li>
  <li><strong>Sponsor a Youth Volunteer:</strong> Support a student, pupil, or anyone under 21. 
      Your contribution covers their access and helps inspire the next generation of engineers.</li>
  <li><strong>Raffle Sponsor:</strong> Provide prizes for our giveaway and ensure everyone remembers 
      your brand when the lucky winners are announced.</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>Food &amp; Drinks (Silver or Gold Sponsors)</h2>
<p><strong>As we all know, the best conversations happen over a meal (or a coffee).</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Breakfast &amp; Lunch:</strong> Keep the energy high and the ideas flowing.</li>
  <li><strong>Coffee Breaks &amp; Water:</strong> Be the hero of the "hallway track."</li>
  <li><strong>The DevOpsDays Beer:</strong> A fan favorite for networking and winding down.</li>
  <li><strong>Party Day 1:</strong> Host the ultimate social mixer to celebrate our first day.</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>Brand &amp; Recruitment (Silver or Gold Sponsors)</h2>
<p><strong>Get your team front and center with our attendees.</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Gold Sponsor (Premium Booth):</strong> Claim the spotlight with a prime-location booth. 
      This tier is designed for maximum foot traffic, giving you the best vantage point for 
      deep-dive product demos and high-volume recruitment.</li>
  <li><strong>Silver Sponsor (Standard Booth):</strong> The perfect home base for your team. 
      Set up shop, showcase your product, and meet the community face-to-face in a high-energy environment.</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<p>
    <strong>
We love surprising our participants, and we especially love it when our sponsors share giveaway gifts with them! You can place your gifts on our special table each day, and we kindly ask that you collect any remaining items at the end of the day. Sharing in this way spreads joy and shows your love for our waste-conscious community.
    </strong>
</p>

<p>
  <strong>Ready to join us?</strong> Every sponsor helps make DevOpsDays Sibiu more accessible and impactful. 
  If any of these options spark your interest, or if you’d like to discuss a custom package, let’s chat!
</p>

<p>
  We look forward to seeing you there.
</p>

<p>
  Best regards,<br>
  <strong>The DevOpsDays Sibiu Team</strong>
</p>
