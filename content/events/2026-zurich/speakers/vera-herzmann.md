Vera Herzmann is a systemic organizational development consultant, podcast host, and advocate for neurodiversity in the workplace. With over eight years of experience, she guides organizations through transformation and innovation, currently serving as Senior Consulting Manager at Zühlke. As a highly sensitive person, she hosts the podcast «zartkraft», where she explores Neurosensitivity and Neuromanagement in both personal and professional contexts. She holds a master’s in Management, Organization Studies & Cultural Theory from the University of St. Gallen. Before joining Zühlke, she specialized in New Work and self-organization, with experience in holacratic organizations shaping her career.
