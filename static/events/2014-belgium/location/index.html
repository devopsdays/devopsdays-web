Travelling & Hotels

The location of the event is slightly of the centre of Ghent. Therefore any hotel in the centre will be fine. Either take public transport or a taxi.

We recommend the Mariott Ghent hotel as the 'conferecence' hotel, but you're of course free to stay at any other location.
