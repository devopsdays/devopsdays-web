Getting There

The venue is a short 10 minute walk from the CBD across the Brisbane River via the Victoria Bridge

Bus - Regular bus services run via the Cultural Centre or South Brisbane Busway Stations (5 minute walk).

Train - South Brisbane Railway Station is adjacent to the complex on Grey Street.

Car Park - A 1,500 vehicle paid car park is available at the centre. There are also nearby alternative paid car parks at South Bank and the Queensland Performing Arts Complex.
