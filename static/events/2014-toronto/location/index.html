Conference location:

DevOps Days Toronto is being hosted at the The Burroughes Building near the corner of Queen and Bathurst. The event is taking place on the 6th floor, with access to the rooftop terrace.

The Burroughes: 639 Queen St West, Toronto, ON. M5V 2B7 - (416) 360-5757


View Larger Map

Public Transportation

The Burroughes Building is easily accessible by public transit. Visit the Toronto Transit Commission (TTC)'s website for transit directions or use Google Maps as a starting point for finding transit information. Note, cash fare for one trip is $3.

Planes, Trains and Automobiles

Toronto has two airports: Toronto Pearson International Airport and Porter Airlines. Check Expedia.ca for flight information.

Via Rail and GO Transit trains connect at Union Station, the main station for Toronto. Union Station connects with public transit.

The closest parking lot near The Burroughes Building is Green P's Carpark 106 at 15 Dension Avenue. Daytime rates (7am - 6pm) is $10.00 and night time rates (6pm - 7am) are $6.00.

Accommodations

Toronto is a world class city with a multitude of accomodation options. Please visit TripAdvisor to view available hotels while you attend DevOps Days Toronto.

Tourism

Come to Toronto for DevOps Days, but check Tourism Toronto for more information on tourist packages and hotels. Sometimes a package deal can make travel less expensive and more fun. What is your interest: opera, ballet, live theatre, art gallery, museums, shopping, music, sports?
