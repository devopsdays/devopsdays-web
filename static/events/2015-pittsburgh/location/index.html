Conference Location

William Pitt Union, University of Pittsburgh



View Larger Map

WiFi will not be provided by the conference. We will provide stickers, hats, or some other easily noticeable marker available that people who are sharing their WiFi can wear so that folks actually have to talk to fellow attendees to use their WiFi. Please considering bringing your own HotSpot or tether; sharing is caring!

There will be a work room that have WiFi available for free through Pitt. There is a required sign-up process that requires access to your email before getting on the WiFi, so you will need access to your email on your smartphone or some other way.

We note the irony of convincing people to share WiFi at a security-minded conference: please prepare ahead of time.
