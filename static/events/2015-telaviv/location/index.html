Event Location:

DevOpsDays TLV is being hosted at the Wohl Center, which is located across the north campus of Bar Ilan University.

Wohl Center: Max ve-Anna Webb St, Ramat Gan, Israel



Arriving by bus

  • From TLV central bus station bus lines: 68, 70, 350, 472
  • From Tel Aviv-Savidor Center train station bus lines: 70, 69
  • From other locations Wohl Center is located near Bar Ilan University. You can take any bus that stops near by and walk to Wohl Center (10-20 minutes).

Arriving by car

From highway number 4 take the exit to Bar Ilan intechange towards Bar Ilan University. Keep on the right lane and do not go into the tunnel. Parking

There are a number of paid parking lots near Wohl Center. Main parking lot entrance is from the roundabout next to tge Wohl Center entrance. Parking price is 15.5 NIS for the entire day. Please notice that your entrance ticket doesn't include parking.
