devopsdays Washington, DC - location

DevOpsDays DC 2016 was held at The United States Patent and Trademark Office’s Madison Auditorium at 600 Dulany Street, Alexandria, Virginia, just outside the District (but inside the Beltway!) Please see USPTO’s directions page for driving directions and parking information.

View the site layout to see the sponsor tables, registration, and lunch area.

Local Hotels

There are a number of hotels near the US Patent and Trademark Office. Some options include:

  • The Westin Alexandria - official hotel of DevOpsDays DC.
  • Residence Inn Alexandria Old Town/Duke Street
  • Hilton Alexandria Old Town
  • Wyndham Old Town Alexandria

DC and Northern Virginia traffic is consistently rated among the worst in the nation, so we recommend that you do not drive, especially if you are visiting from out of town. Instead, take Metro to the event. USPTO is located a few blocks from the King Street/Old Town station, which is served by Blue and Yellow line trains. It’s also a short ride from National Airport (DCA), our recommended airport for visitors from out of town. If you’re arriving at Dulles (IAD), Baltimore (BWI), or Amtrak’s Union Station, Metro’s trip planner can help you get to the event.
