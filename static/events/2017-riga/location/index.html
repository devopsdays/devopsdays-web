devopsdays Riga - Event Location

The conference is located at TALLINK HOTEL RIGA, Elizabetes iela 24, Riga, LV-1050

Getting there and accommodations

DevOpsDays Riga 2017 attendees have a special price at Tallink Hotel Riga - €69,00 for night

Tallink hotel is located near the Riga Central Railway station and less than a kilometer away from International Bus station.

Getting to the hotel from Riga International Airport (RIX) is a little bit more tricky, however, it is possible to use No. 22 bus that leaves from RIX every 10-30 minutes. Suggested stop to get off at is "Autoosta". From there, just walk down the street, past the railway station and shopping center "Origo", until you reach Tallink hotel. Check out this Google Maps for travel options.
Check out Riga International Airport Public transport section for more details for getting to Riga center from RIX

Transportation

Transportation for DevOpsDays Riga 2017 is provided by official transportation sponsor Taxify

Alternatively, there are multiple cabs that you can grab at any time, as well as good public transport system. Check out Rigas Satiksme page for public transport map. Bicycles can be rented at any time from Sixt bike racks, that are located all over the city.

Parking

Parking on the streets

Paid parking spots are available on the streets within few minutes' walk to the hotel. Check Rigas Satiksme map of streets, that have available parking spots near the venue. Parking on the streets in Riga costs about € 2/3 per one hour (B/A zones).

Underground parking and car parks

Cheapest and closest Europark parking lot is located at Dzirnavu iela 123, Rīga. This parking costs € 1,5 per hour or € 4,5 for period from 8AM until 8PM (12h).

Underground parking is availabe close to the hotel in "Origo" shopping center, located at Stacijas laukums 4, Rīga. Car entrance from Satekles street. This parking costs € 2 per hour or € 10 per 24 hours.

Another option is to use parking in multistory car park next to the Riga Central Market - see UniPark Prāgas 2 car park page for prices and details. Please note that parking here costs € 2 per hour and/or € 40 per day.
