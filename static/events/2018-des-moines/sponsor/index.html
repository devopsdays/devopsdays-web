devopsdays Des Moines - Sponsor

We greatly value sponsors for this open event. If you are interested in sponsoring, please drop us an email.

devopsdays is a self-organizing conference for practitioners that depends on sponsorships. We do not have vendor booths, sell product presentations, or distribute attendee contact lists. Sponsors will get recognition on the website and social media before, during and after the event. Sponsors are encouraged to represent themselves by actively participating and engaging with the attendees as peers. Any attendee also has the opportunity to demo products/projects as part of an open space session.

All attendees are welcome to propose any subject they want during the open spaces, but this is a community-focused conference, so heavy marketing will probably work against you when trying to make a good impression on the attendees.

The best thing to do is send engineers to interact with the experts at devopsdays on their own terms.

Sponsor FAQ Answers to questions frequently asked by sponsors
What dates/times can we set up and tear down? Sponsors will have time to setup an hour before registration on the first day of the event.
How do we ship to the venue? Please contact us organizers-des-moines-2018@devopsdays.org
Whom should we send? Please send somebody that can tell your story and participate with the community.
What should we expect regarding electricity? (how much, any fees, etc) During setup the facilities staff will do their best to setup extension cords for you. Please contact us so we can set expectations with the venue staff.
What should we expect regarding WiFi? (how much, any fees, etc) WiFi will be included at the event. No additional cost.
How do we order additional A/V equipment? Please contact us organizers-des-moines-2018@devopsdays.org
